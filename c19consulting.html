Writing & Research 2023—

C19 Consulting is the practice of Anaïs Cyana-Djoher Hadjali — consultant, researcher, and writer working at the intersection of fashion, art, and archives.

Close attention, precise description, care.
About

Founded in Paris, C19 works with brands, institutions, and platforms on creative strategy, art direction, production, and editorial projects. The practice is grounded in doctoral research on the fashion design gesture (Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne / Università di Bologna), and treats every engagement with the method of the archive: close attention, precise description, care.

Work is conducted in English and French, from Paris and wherever the project requires — most recently Venice, Milan, New York, and Tokyo.
